Welcome to Kitty Haven Malaysia At Kitty Haven Malaysia, we believe that every cat deserves love, care, and a safe home. Founded with the mission to improve the lives of stray cats across Malaysia, our organization focuses on healthcare, protection, and well-being for these vulnerable animals, while working tirelessly to find them loving, permanent homes.

Our Mission Our mission is to create a compassionate community where stray cats are no longer neglected but embraced as cherished companions. We aim to: - Provide Healthcare: Our team ensures that every rescued cat receives proper medical attention, including vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and treatment for injuries or illnesses. - Offer Protection: We provide a safe and nurturing environment in our shelters, protecting cats from the dangers of life on the streets. - Promote Well-Being: Our goal is to rehabilitate each cat emotionally and physically, preparing them for a loving home.

Finding Forever Homes Our adoption program is dedicated to pairing rescued cats with responsible and caring families. We carefully assess each potential adopter to ensure they can provide the love and stability that every cat deserves. By adopting from us, you're not only giving a cat a new chance at life but also helping reduce the stray cat population in Malaysia. Why It Matters Stray cats in Malaysia often face harsh conditions, including starvation, injuries, and disease. By rescuing and rehabilitating these animals, we aim to break the cycle of suffering and show that every life is worth saving.

How You Can Help 1. Adopt: Open your heart and home to one of our rescue cats. 2. Donate: Your support helps us cover medical costs, food, and shelter for the cats. 3. Volunteer: Join our team to make a hands-on difference in their lives. 4. Spread the Word: Help us reach more people who share our passion for saving lives. Together, we can create a brighter future for Malaysia's stray cats.

Thank you for being a part of this journey! - The Kitty Haven Malaysia Team
